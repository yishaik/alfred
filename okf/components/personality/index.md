# Concepts

* [dream.py — overnight brief](dream.md) - Builds a morning brief (yesterday's recap + today's agenda) by reusing the digest and scheduled jobs.
* [mood.py — transient mood](mood.md) - A lightweight transient mood (recovering/cautious/weary/in-the-zone/neutral) layered on the stable soul.
* [proactive.py — idle check-ins](proactive.md) - Autonomous "speak up only if there's a genuine open loop" turns, gated by quiet hours and turn budget.
* [soul.py — character sheet](soul.md) - Structured persona (name, emoji, role, tone, values, quirks) injected into every turn's system prompt.
